Aniket's Brain
2019-10-05

Hey guys, hope all is will with you all. If there is anyone actually reading this….

Didnt think that I’d ever write on thing thing again, but I guess I should give it a shot :).

Lately my thoughts have been wandering to where the next technological advancement will come from.
We seem to have reached a point where most of the new software companies are providing an incremental improvement to everyday life, which is in fairly stark comparison to what we saw in about 2000-15.
I’d like to thing our ability to improve the mobile devices we already have will increase drastically, but given the fact that all brands of phones are essentially the same device with different software, my hope in that field is limited till we shift from the paradigm of touch sensitive phones.
Maybe, it will have to do with the massive research going into handling the exobytes of data that is to come in the near future from all sorts of different sources.
Lately it seems like all the hot topics are related to “machine learning” and what it can provide to businesses in terms of improving decision making.
My first foray into that field, particularly computer vision, made me feel that we are at a point where we can generalize most things with machine learning, but we still are not at point of extracting causation.
Rather, we are providing some very fine grain correlation the likes of which compete with our own brains ability to compare like items.
At this point I have to think that we are essentially training computers to confirm what the data is telling us is what we are thinking of the data is right or wrong.
At this point I feel like I’ve sufficiently confused anyone who may have stumbled onto this post and will be going to sleep. Maybe I’ll be able to make sense of this one day.
